The Problem

Over 60% of Tunisian adults remain excluded from the formal banking system, making it impossible to finance a vehicle — the single most impactful asset for income generation — trapping low-income workers in a cycle of poverty and immobility that costs the Tunisian economy billions of dinars in lost productivity each year.

FIN'IX is an all-in-one digital platform that combines micro-loans and micro-insurance to give unbanked Tunisians fast, personalized vehicle financing — fully accessible from a smartphone, no bank branch required.

Why FIN'IX?

FIN'IX is the first platform in Tunisia that combines micro-credit and micro-insurance in a single digital ecosystem — purpose-built for the unbanked. Unlike traditional lenders or standalone apps, FIN'IX removes every barrier simultaneously: no credit history required, no branch visit, and built-in protection for both lender and borrower.
